What is CityMind?

CityMind is an AI-powered infrastructure health intelligence platform. It processes aerial drone footage of buildings and structures through a 6-layer pipeline — from video ingestion to AI-generated compliance reports.

The system uses multi-agent reasoning (Inspector, Compliance, Safety) to automatically classify defects, assess structural risk, and generate actionable maintenance reports — all running entirely on-device.

AMD Technology

Ryzen AI

CityMind is built for the AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370 with XDNA2 NPU (50 TOPS):

  • YOLOv8 & RT-DETR — INT4 quantized via Vitis AI
  • LLM agent inference (Llama 3.1, Mistral 7B) on NPU
  • 3.5× perception speedup vs CPU baseline
  • Full pipeline in 18.7s — no cloud required
