Problem

Infrastructure deterioration is a $2.6 trillion global crisis. Manual inspection is slow (4-8 hrs), expensive ($2K-$10K), dangerous, and subjective (40% disagreement between inspectors).

The construction industry loses $150B+ annually to rework from late-detected defects.

Solution

CityMind transforms a 30-second phone video into a 3D digital twin with automated defect detection, building code compliance via multi-agent AI, and executive reports.

Runs 100% offline on AMD Ryzen AI — 90% faster, 90% cheaper than manual inspection.
