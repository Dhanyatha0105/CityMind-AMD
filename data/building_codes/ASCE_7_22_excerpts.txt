CHAPTER 11 — SEISMIC DESIGN CRITERIA

11.4 Seismic Ground Motion Values
  Spectral acceleration parameters: SS and S1 from USGS database.
  Site coefficients Fa and Fv based on site class.

11.6 Seismic Design Category
  Structures are assigned to Seismic Design Categories A through F based on
  their Risk Category and the design spectral acceleration parameters.

CHAPTER 12 — SEISMIC DESIGN REQUIREMENTS FOR BUILDING STRUCTURES

12.8 Equivalent Lateral Force Procedure
  V = Cs × W (seismic base shear)
  Cs = SDS / (R/Ie) (seismic response coefficient)

12.12.1 Story Drift Limits
  Risk Category I or II: 0.020hsx
  Risk Category III: 0.015hsx
  Risk Category IV: 0.010hsx

CHAPTER 26 — WIND LOADS

26.5 Wind Speed Maps
  Basic wind speed shall be determined from maps based on Risk Category.
  Exposure categories: B (urban/suburban), C (open terrain), D (flat/water).

CHAPTER 4 — LIVE LOADS

Table 4.3-1: Minimum Uniformly Distributed Live Loads
  Assembly areas: 4.8 kN/m² (fixed seats), 4.8 kN/m² (movable seats)
  Office buildings: 2.4 kN/m²
  Residential: 1.9 kN/m²
  Parking garages: 2.4 kN/m²
  Storage (heavy): 11.97 kN/m²

CHAPTER 15 — STRUCTURAL INTEGRITY

15.2 General Structural Integrity Requirements
  Structures shall be designed to sustain local damage without disproportionate collapse.
  Tie force requirements for multi-story buildings.
